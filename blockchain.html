Blockchain

A block chain is a group of information collected together forming a block but they can only storge so much so they get connected to the previous filled
block creating a chain of data and when new data is added a new block is formed and connected this process repeats and continues indefinitely there are two
main forms of blockchains private and public, Private where there is a single entry and can control membership usually used for business
network and public where anyone can join validate their transaction. Blockchains make a tamper proof ledge of all transactions made but it not completely
safe can still be attack through fraud and cyberattacks routing, phishing, stolen keys and code exploitation to name a few, but all these methods take a lot of
time and rely heavily on human error to succeed. The main use of block chain now a days is for crypto currency which is a
private decentralization blockchain, bitcoins need multiple computers to store their blockchains.
The blockchain used bitcoins is a data base of all the transaction made but what make this different for other blockchains and decentralized
is the fact the computers used are not all in the same locations and are controls / operated by a group of people or different individuals.
A good way to think about it is a company would keep all its info on a group of serves in one place but with bitcoin
the info is store over thousands of computers from all over the worlds forming a network the computers making up the network are called nodes.
Each of these nodes are full of the data for all the trades and transactions. Having all these nodes means that if one has an error,
it can correct itself by referring to all the others. Having the ability to self-correcting its code is very useful.

Bitcoin open the path laid the groundwork for many other new and exciting block chains:

These are just a of the new developments happening right now at right now in the field of block chains I could write about any of these but
the one I will focus on will be Central Bank Digital Currency.
CBDC is heavily inspired but bitcoin but differs from crypto and virtual current by being issued by a state
can be used as legal tender right now CBDC is a proof of concept but around 80% of centrals are interested digital currencies
with China being the first to release one with RMB. A CBDC would likely happen by central bank, government or a privatized
sector using a data base controlled by them keeping a record how much money a person or a company has.
Another way it differs form a crypto currency would that it could be centrally control lessening the data need in it blockchain.
CBDC would be highly security and just like paper money a unique identical feature to stop it from being counterfeited.

transactions could be made in real time meaning less risk for the sellers by having to waiting period between purchase and transfer of funds,
with no need for the to track of complex transaction in with the customer says they paid but the money has not gone through yet.
illegal acts could easily be traced, and trade and tax evasion would be way more difficult to do due to unreported employment,
offshore bank account and money laundering all now having a clear trail of transaction and the money
could return to anyone affected by these acts or any scams really. a record would provide a ledger to help with complicated tasks inheritance,
