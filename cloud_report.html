Cloud services and servers

What Does it Do? (600 words)

Cloud services and servers is a very important widely used technology in our modern-day life.
Companies use cloud-based services for several reasons, but what are they and how are they useful?
If a company wanted to set up a computer system at their location they have a few options,
they can always buy all the physical equipment themselves and have everything running in-house;
but there would be a large upfront cost to buy all that equipment not to mention the time it would take to get it all set up for use.
Therefore, a lot of companies like to use cloud computing services and servers.
If a company decides to go the cloud route, they do not have to buy anywhere near as much equipment
up-from and setup times for the system are extremely less than if they were to do it themselves.
With cloud-based systems, companies will pay a cloud services company to do all the work for them for the computer systems they need,
this means no or very little on site setup and next to no maintenance for their systems,
making cloud servers and services a no-brainer for most companies.
Now we have talked about why it is a good idea for most companies to use cloud servers and services, let us look at some more examples of cloud-based systems.

How do most companies store their data?
If you are a company that must store a lot of data, a server is what you need. Sometimes companies will have in-house servers,
meaning the server that they use to back-up and store all their data is physically inside the location of the company or in some cases its own building entirely.
This can be very expensive doing it this way as again you have all the upfront costs of buying all the equipment the physically build the system,
onto of that you are also using that space for the servers that could alternatively be used for other reason thus making in-house servers physically inefficient.
Using cloud-based servers for you company is a very cost-efficient alternative to in-house server systems,
companies will pay cloud server companies to host their server for them therefore eliminating the cost of setup and maintenance
on their end as well as the space needed to physically have the servers installed and running; not to mention the money that is saved on the power bills.

Data loss

If a company has in-house servers for all their data, they will always be risking data loss. What happens if the hard drives fail?
What if someone breaks in and steals some hard drives? What happens if there is a natural disaster such as a flood or a fire that ruins the system? The data is lost.
Therefore, companies love to use cloud-based servers to store their data, most cloud server providers have servers all over the world,
or at least in multiple locations. If there is ever and issue with a location or hard drive going down the data is already backed-up somewhere
else in another one of the locations that the provider has server’s setup. This is a very important and appealing perk if going with a cloud server provider for a company,
knowing that they will not have to deal or worry about loss of data gives companies one less thing to worry about.

Maintenance

If a company has an in-house server setup then they are required to perform their own maintenance on their server system however,
cloud server providers take care of the maintenance for their systems so customers never need to worry about maintaining a server system for their company.

What is the likely impact (300 words)

Cloud services and servers has a huge impact on small businesses and companies, even person use.
Using cloud-based services has a massive positive financial impact on individuals, businesses, and companies alike.
Let us look again at servers, there are huge cost benefits to not having to physically install and maintain in-house servers,
no upfront costs of buying all the equipment and having to have someone install it all and set it all up.
Maintaining your own system for a large company can be very expensive and frustrating if you do not have the required expertise in your company,
because of this most companies must assign or hire staff just to maintain their systems or at least hire contractors to come in and fix a problem
or update a system for them, costing the company more money every time something needs to be done.

In general, the world is moving away from local computing all together and moving towards having most of their systems and services based in the cloud.
This is having a fantastic impact on how easy it can be to setup systems in general for companies and everyone making starting and building your own business
must easier as far as the systems needed for storing your data, plus much more.
Having systems based in the cloud is also very good for travelling and being able to access the same data or systems that you would usually
need to be in your location of operations to access, being able to access all your data and your computer systems from anywhere in the world is a game changer
for the industry and any industry that requires accessing data and systems from more than one location.

Imagine the financial impact that it would have if people had to setup new systems at each physical location that you wanted to expand to,
with cloud-based services you don’t have to worry about it as everything you need is already accessible no matter where you are.
